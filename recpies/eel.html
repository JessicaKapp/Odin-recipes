Eel Sauce

Perfect for Sushi

This simple sauce only needs three ingredients and comes together in as little as 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce

Preparation

  1. Add ingredients to a small saucepan on medium heat. stir continuously until desired thickness is reached.
  2. Pour into glass or ceramic bowl and allow to cool. Serve over your favorite sushi and enjoy!

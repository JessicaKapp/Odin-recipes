Soy Sauce Chicken Thighs

Crunchy Salty Chicken Thighs

I've been making this recipe since I was in college. Its so easy, delicious, and affordable!

My favorite way of cooking this is in the air fryer, but cooking it old fashion style in the oven works just as well. It just takes longer.

Ingredients

  • Four bone-in, skin on chicken thighs
  • 3/4 cup of low sodium soy sauce

Preparation

  1. Clean and prep your chicken thighs. Add them to a small bowl or a resealable freezer bag, then pour in the soy sauce. Allow to marinated in the fridge for at least four hours.
  2. Preheat the air fryer to 390 then add the Chicken thighs. cook for 26-28 minutes depending on how crispy you like the skins.
  3. If you would rather use a traditional oven, preheat to 425. Add the chicken thighs to a lightly greased dish and cook for one hour and 15 minutes.
  4. Serve with white rice and enjoy!
